About Us

The Directorate of Economics & Statistics (DES) is dedicated to collecting, analyzing, and disseminating statistical data for the state of Tripura. Our mission is to provide accurate and timely statistics to support informed decision-making and policy development.

Our Vision

To be a leading statistical agency known for the integrity, reliability, and quality of our data and services.
